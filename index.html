Our Founder's Journey

As founder and creative director of Gboy Atelier, I spent fifteen years apprenticing with master craftsmen across Milan, Paris, and Tokyo before establishing our atelier in 2022. My background in architectural design and textile innovation shapes our distinctive aesthetic—structured silhouettes with unexpected material combinations that honor traditional techniques while pushing creative boundaries.

The Craft Behind Each Creation

Gboy Atelier represents the renaissance of thoughtful luxury in an age of disposable fashion. Each piece in our collection undergoes a rigorous 37-step creation process, combining time-honored craftsmanship with contemporary innovation.

Our footwear is handcrafted by a small team of artisans who train for a minimum of seven years before touching a client commission. We source vegetable-tanned leathers exclusively from heritage tanneries that practice sustainable water management and ethical animal husbandry. Gboy signature footwear features hand-carved wooden lasts created specifically for each client, ensuring perfect fit and unparalleled comfort that improves with wear.

Our apparel collection employs fabrics developed through exclusive partnerships with world-renowned mills—from water-repellent sea island cotton to cashmere blended with recycled silver thread for temperature regulation. Each garment features hand-rolled edges, pick-stitched lapels, and our proprietary internal construction that allows for natural movement while maintaining structural integrity.

The Bespoke Experience

The Gboy Atelier experience begins with a personal consultation, either in our flagship atelier or virtually for international clients. Our master craftspeople measure extensively and discuss lifestyle requirements before creating an individualized design proposal. Clients participate in two fittings to ensure perfect execution. Each Gboy creation is delivered in our signature packaging with detailed care instructions and lifetime maintenance service.
